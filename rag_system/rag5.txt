
University of Calicut
B.Sc Applied Statistics with Data Science (Honours)
CUFYUGP Regulations 2024
(Implemented from 2024 admission onwards)

PROGRAMME STRUCTURE:
---------------------
- Pathways: Single Major, Major with Minor, Major with Multiple Disciplines
- UG Degree (3 Years): 133 Credits
- UG Honours Degree (4 Years): 177 Credits
- Components: Major, Minor, Foundation, Ability & Skill Enhancement, Value Added, Internship, Project

PROGRAMME OUTCOMES (PO):
-------------------------
- PO1: Knowledge Acquisition
- PO2: Communication, Collaboration, Inclusiveness, Leadership
- PO3: Professional Skills
- PO4: Digital Intelligence
- PO5: Scientific Awareness & Critical Thinking
- PO6: Human Values, Ethics, Societal & Environmental Responsibility
- PO7: Research, Innovation & Entrepreneurship

PROGRAMME SPECIFIC OUTCOMES (PSO):
-----------------------------------
- PSO1: Statistical Concepts, Principles, and Applications
- PSO2: Descriptive & Inferential Statistics via Data Science
- PSO3: Proficiency in Statistical Software
- PSO4: Identify Applications of Statistical Theories
- PSO5: Construct Models for Real-World Problems
- PSO6: Lifelong Learning of Relevant Skills
- PSO7: Industry-Ready Statistical Competence

CREDIT DISTRIBUTION:
---------------------
Three-Year Program (133 Credits):
- Major: 68 (17 courses)
- Minor: 24 (6 courses)
- Foundation (AEC, MDC, SEC, VAC) (Value Added, 39
- Internship: 2

Four-Year Program (177 Credits):
- Additional Major (7th Sem): 20
- Additional Major/Minor or Project (8th Sem): 24 or 12 (Project)
- Total: 177

SEMESTER-WISE STRUCTURE:
==========================

- Minor Course 2 (4 credit)

- MDC 1 (e.g., Quality Control/Fundamentals of Statistics) (3 credit)

SEMESTER 2 (21 Credits)
-------------------------

- Minor Course 3 (4 credit)
- Minor Course 4 (4 credit)
- AEC 3 (English) (3 credit)
- AEC 4 (Additional Language) (3 credit)
- MDC 2 (e.g., Managerial Decision Making/Statistical Sampling) (3 credit)

SEMESTER 3 (22 Credits)
--------------------------

SEMESTER 4 (21 Credits)
-------------------------

SEMESTER 5 (23 Credits)

SEMESTER 6 (25 Credits)

SEMESTER 7 – Honours Only (20 Credits)

SEMESTER 8 – Honours Only (24 Credits)
------------------------------------------

  - STA8CJ408: Generalized Linear Models (4 credit)
Option 2 – Project Path:
  - STA8CJ449 / STA8CJ499: Project / Research Project (12 credit)

Both Options:
  - Elective 5 (4 credit)
  - Elective 6 (4 credit)
  - Elective 7 or Research Methodology (4 credit)

ELECTIVES:
----------
Semester 5:
  - Official Statistics, Actuarial Statistics, Psychometric Theory, Survival Analysis, Population Statistics

Semester 6:
  - Biostatistics, Econometrics, Statistical Quality Control, Operations Research, Simulation Techniques

Semester 7:
  - Machine Learning Methods, Operations Research, Queueing Models, Decision Theory, Clinical Trials,
    Applied Algorithms & Big Data, Advanced Trends in Statistics

ASSESSMENT:
------------
- Internal: 30% (Mid-terms, Assignments, Practicals)
- External: 70% (Structured Exams)
- Grading: 10-point scale (O to F), SGPA, CGPA based

SPECIAL REQUIREMENTS:
----------------------
Internship (2 credit, min 60 hrs):
- Supervised, report submission, viva-voce, institutional evaluation

Project (12 credit, min 300 hrs):
- Supervised individual work (theoretical/computational)
- Evaluation: Internal 30%, Viva 50%, Report 20%
- Honours with Research: Requires PhD faculty supervision

SOFTWARE TRAINING:
-------------------
- R and Python integrated across practicals and coursework
